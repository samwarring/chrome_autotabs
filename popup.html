Alternate Domain Names

Alter the domain name of a tab before sorting and grouping.

Provide a pattern and an alternate domain name. Tabs whose urls match the pattern will be sorted and grouped according to their alternate domain name.

The pattern must be a valid regular expression, and is matched against the entire URL (host and path). The replacement must be a valid domain name.
